# Bike_Sales_Dataset_Excel
In this project, data cleaning and data exploration are done on the bike sales dataset using Excel.
## Findings and Insights
Pivot tables are used to answer some of the questions, such as 
- Pivot table shows that even though the average income of males is higher than females, females purchase bikes more.
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1d5fbd60-8469-4974-ac4b-2a90593392e7)
- The total purchase of bikes is higher for married people than for single people. Married females purchase more than married males.
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e5e948b9-42b2-4e7a-87e9-65920c472eda)
- Pivot table displays that customers use bikes to commute shorter distances.
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c1a99647-94f9-436b-99ab-8f3cebf04ea9)
- It illustrates that middle-aged people buy more bikes than people of old age.
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/eb270616-7943-4ea2-bed8-3237a948172a)
- Pivot table shows age between 34 to 37 buys the highest bikes. And as the age increases, bike purchase lessens.
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6430a168-8e97-4894-a6ac-35f40c6e64da)
